05. Corner Radius Analysis

16px Radius — Right or Wrong?

The current system uses a single corner radius of 16px for all buttons. Here's how that compares.

System Button Radius Approach
Material Design 320dp (full capsule)Single capsule shape
Apple HIG12pt / capsuleVaries by context
Ant Design Mobile4px / 8px / capsuleMultiple radius tokens
Carbon0pxSharp corners (brand decision)
Atlassian3pxSubtle rounding
Finding: The Partner App needs sharper corners — it's a professional, utility-focused tool and should feel functional, not playful. The Customer App can afford more rounded, friendlier shapes. However, since both apps share a single CTA component system, the radius needs to work for both contexts. 16px is too rounded for the Partner side, and anything below 8px would feel too sharp for the Consumer side. A radius around 12px is the sweet spot — professional enough for partners, approachable enough for consumers, and aligned with Apple HIG's 12pt standard.
