05. The 9 Universal Rules

Non-Negotiable Standards

  • 1
    Max 2 CTAs — never 3 on any screen, sheet, or dialog.
  • 2
    Primary appears only once per screen / sheet / dialog.
  • 3
    Right = preferred action in side-by-side layout.
  • 4
    Top = preferred action in vertical stack layout.
  • 5
    Tertiary only in vertical stacks — never side by side with Primary. Borderless next to filled looks broken horizontally.
  • 6
    Destructive never alone — always paired with a Secondary escape route.
  • 7
    CTA text = next screen's context — "भुगतान करें" (Pay) on plan selection, not "आगे बढ़ें" (Continue). Reduces anxiety for partners.
  • 8
    Acknowledge uses 1st person — "मैंने सभी जानकारी सही दी है" not "कस्टमर ने...". Forces personal accountability.
  • 9
    CTA container = flat, no shadow — border-top only using color.border.default. No box-shadow, no elevation, no exceptions.
